John F. Wesseling, PhD

Principal Investigator
Dr. Wesseling received a BS in Symbolic Systems from Stanford University in 1990, then a PhD in Neuroscience from the Salk Institute/UCSD in 1997. His main scientific interest is in developing a theoretical framework for the dynamic changes in connection strength that occur at chemical synapses during repetitive activation.
